Privacy Notice

Taiko Quest NATC Phoenix 2025 • Taiko Community Alliance (TCA)

Last updated: August 18, 2025

This Privacy Notice explains how Taiko Community Alliance (“TCA”, “we”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Taiko Quest NATC Phoenix 2025 website and related pages (the “Service”). This notice works together with our Terms of Use.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, and basic profile data created during sign-in (Google for admins; email magic link for attendees).
  • Submissions: photos/videos you upload to complete Quest Board items, plus related metadata (timestamps, file URLs, item IDs).
  • Usage: basic event logs (e.g., sign-in/out, success/failure of uploads) for security and troubleshooting.
  • Device/technical: standard web logs (IP address, browser/OS info) collected by our hosting/providers for reliability and security.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide the Service (Quest Board creation, uploads, verification, and your ability to revisit your board).
  • To review and verify Submissions (admins may mark items “Verified”).
  • To promote and document TCA activities, consistent with the consent/license you accept in the Terms.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve reliability of the Service.

3) Legal Basis (if applicable)

Where required by law (e.g., in the EEA/UK), we rely on your consent (for media use) and our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and documenting a community arts event. You can request withdrawal of consent for future use of specific Submissions (see Your Rights), noting that past use already published may not be retractable.

4) Sharing & Service Providers

We use trusted providers to operate the Service. They process data on our behalf under their terms:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, file storage for uploads).
  • GitHub Pages (website hosting for these static pages).
  • Google (OAuth for admin sign-in; TCA Google Workspace for archival of media after the event).

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety and rights.

5) Retention

We retain account data and Submissions for the duration of the event and for a reasonable period afterward to support documentation, archival, and mission-related media use. Media may be archived to TCA’s Google Workspace. We may periodically reduce or remove copies from active Supabase storage after archiving. Aggregated, de-identified data may be kept longer.

6) Security

We use platform-level protections provided by Supabase and GitHub Pages (e.g., managed infrastructure, role-based access, row-level security). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please avoid uploading sensitive personal information.

7) Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for participants aged 13 and over. Submissions featuring minors require appropriate consent from a parent/guardian. Do not upload images/videos of minors without such consent.

8) Cookies & Local Storage

  • Auth tokens: Supabase uses local/session storage to keep you signed in.
  • Basic preferences: We may store small flags (e.g., UI state) in your browser for convenience.
  • You can clear these via your browser settings; doing so may sign you out.

9) International Transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States and other countries. By using the Service, you understand your data may be transferred to locations with different data protection laws than your home jurisdiction.

10) Your Rights & Choices

  • Access/Correction: Request a copy of your information or corrections to inaccuracies.
  • Removal Requests: Ask us to remove specific Submissions where feasible. Prior published uses may not be retractable.
  • Consent: You may withdraw consent for future media use; we’ll respect this going forward where practical.
  • Opt-out: You can choose not to upload media and still participate where permitted by event rules.

To submit a request, contact us (see Contact). We may ask for information to verify your identity and respond within a reasonable period, subject to applicable law.

11) Links to Other Sites

Our pages may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies when you visit them.

12) Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date above and, where feasible, provide an in-app notice for material changes. Your continued use after updates signifies acceptance.
